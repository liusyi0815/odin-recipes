Grandma's Cornbread Dressing

Use your favorite cornbread mix to make a batch of cornbread

and then crumble it for use in this family-favorite recipe. I hope you enjoy it as much as I have!

Ingredients(1x)

  • 3 cups crumbled cornbread
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 1 tablespoon dried sage, or more to taste
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

How to Make Cornbread Dressing(Quick overview)

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 7x11-inch baking dish. Place crumbled cornbread in a large bowl.
  2. Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Add onion and celery and sauté until soft, 5 to 7 minutes.
  3. Add sautéed onion and celery to the crumbled cornbread. Stir in chicken stock, eggs, sage, salt, and pepper until well combined. Pour dressing into theprepared baking dish.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until dressing just starts to turn golden brown around the edges, about 30 minutes.
